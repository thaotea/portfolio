PROJECT

Google Calendar Redesign

MY ROLE

UI Designer

SUMMARY

I redesigned Google Calendar's mobile application to suit a student's lifestyle.

OVERVIEW

Google School keeps the features students use most in one place so students can keep organized and maintain a productive and healthy lifestyle.

The app maintains the student's school schedule, extracurriculars, and planned goals in the calendar section. The next upcoming event is listed at top and add notes to each event to have easy access to what you need.

Students can keep track of their goals and tasks for each class in the to-do section. After completing each to-do, students can swipe to check it off and update how many goals and tasks you have left for the day.

Students can maintain their drive with lectures, readings, and homework in the drive section. Tasks can be linked to the specific file for easy access. As the use of technology is increasing in student's life, Google School helps keep track of everything in a simple design and encourage students to stay organized in order to reduce the anxiety and stress.

Above is a design prototype of what Google School would look like as a mobile application with some of the few ideas I had discussed. In the future, I hope to implement a design to make Google School more tablet friendly by adding a type of file that supports styluses for things such as notetaking. In addition, I wanted to make setting push notifications an easier process for upcoming important events such as exams rather than having to add it directly to the calendar students can easily add notes to the already made events for that day and add a push notification to update them when exams are about to come up.

ENDING NOTES

Through this personal project, I gained hands-on experience working with Adobe XD to develop a prototype. I was able to mainly strengthen my UI design skills to make an idea of mine come to life. I hope to eventually iterate on this redesign prototype in the future to improve the quality of life for students.
